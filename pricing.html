FAQ

What do you need to start?

Brand name, logo (if any), text copy, images/videos (or I source royalty-free), and 2–3 websites you like as inspiration.

Can you host or buy the domain?

I’ll guide you with domain + hosting setup, or configure GitHub Pages/Netlify for static sites. Costs are billed to you directly.

What’s included in each package?

Basic = 1 page. Starter = 3 pages. Pro = up to 6 pages. Premium = up to 12 pages. All include responsive design, animations, and contact form. Hosting/domain and e-commerce are separate.

Payment terms?

50% upfront, 50% on delivery. Maintenance is billed monthly and can be canceled anytime.
